SeaGL 2013

Oct 11th & 12th

Seattle Central Community College

Room 1110 - Broadway Edison Building
1701 Broadway Seattle, WA 98122

https://SeaGL.org

SeaGL is Seattle's newest grassroots technical conference. We are dedicated to spreading awareness and knowledge about the GNU/Linux ecosystem, Free Software, and the Open Source movement.

Our goal for SeaGL is to produce an event which is both enjoyable and informative for all ranges of skill and experience. We're starting small this year but have managed to pull together a great lineup of volunteer presenters, and we hope that you have a great time while you're here.

The cost of attendance is free.
