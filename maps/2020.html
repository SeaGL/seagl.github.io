Maps & Directions

Conference Venue

Seattle Central College
1701 Broadway
Seattle, WA 98122

Driving Directions

via I-5 N

  1. Take exit 166 (under the Washington State Convention Center) onto Olive Way.
  2. Follow Olive Way and turn right onto Denny Way.
  3. Follow Denny Way up the hill and turn right onto Broadway.
  4. Follow Broadway and turn right onto Pine.
  5. Go one block down to the parking garage located at Harvard & Pine.

via I-5 S

  1. Take exit 166 onto Stewart.
  2. Follow Stewart and turn left onto Denny Way.
  3. Follow Denny Way up the hill and turn right onto Broadway.
  4. Follow Broadway and turn right onto Pine.
  5. Go one block down to the parking garage located at Harvard & Pine.

Parking

There are several private parking lots located near the front entrance at 1701 Broadway Ave. or on Harvard Avenue, which parallels Broadway behind the school. There also is metered 2-hour street parking.

Please use street parking or private parking. SCC's lots are for teachers and students.
