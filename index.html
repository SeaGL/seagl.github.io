Exhibitors

We will have a small hall available for exhibitors. Free tables will be available for free/libre/open-source software/hardware and non-profit organizations, and there are several sponsorship levels for everyone else. If your organization would like an exhibit table at SeaGL, please send an email to {{ site.custom.a.email.sponsor }} for our vendor prospectus.

Exhibitor space is limited, please contact us right away if you are interested in a table.
